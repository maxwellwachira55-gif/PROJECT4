Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to some of the most common questions about insurance claims and working with the firm.

When should I speak to a lawyer about my claim?

As early as possible—ideally before you accept, reject, or sign anything from your insurer. Early advice can prevent costly mistakes and strengthen your position.

What information will I need to share?

Your policy documents, any letters or emails from the insurer, claim forms, photos, medical reports, and any other relevant correspondence or evidence you have.

How are legal fees structured?

Fee arrangements depend on the nature and complexity of your matter. After the initial assessment, you will receive a clear, written explanation of costs before you decide how to proceed.

Can we communicate through WhatsApp?

Yes. WhatsApp is a convenient way to share documents quickly and receive timely updates. Formal communication will also be provided in writing as needed.
